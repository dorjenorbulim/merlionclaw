Heart Sutra & STAGES OF THE PATH (the Six Perfections)

Questions for discussion on Sunday, 29th September 2013

1. (a) Explain how the six perfections  are  definite  in  number  from  the
   point of view of high status.

     b)  Why is there a definite order to the six perfections?

2.    (a)    What is the perfection of generosity?

   (b) How is the perfection of generosity practised by including the  other
   perfections?

3.    (a)     What is the meaning of destruction of roots of virtue?

        (b)  How are the roots of virtue destroyed?

     c) What is the advice given for developing patience?


4. (a) What  are  the  four  favourable  conditions  for  developing  joyous
   effort?

       (b) How does each of them help in the development of joyous effort?
