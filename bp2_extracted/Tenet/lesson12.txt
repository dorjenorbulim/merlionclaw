Proponent of Mind Only is essentially someone who propounds that everything
is in the nature of mind. Having said that, if we look at how thing appear
to our mind, things appear to exist as separate/ different entity from the
mind; as something outside. Basically, what appear to us are as external
objects.
According to MOS, external objects do not exist in reality although there
is an appearance of external objects. Although external objects that are of
different entity/ nature from the mind do not exist, nevertheless they
appear. Why do they appear? Because the mind being completely habituated to
ignorance. Due to the force of ignorance, the latencies of such ignorance
are placed on the mind. Because of these latencies of ignorance, then there
is a projection of external objects. So the external objects appear because
of the latencies of ignorance.
According to the Proponent of Sutra, in the case of the eye consciousness
apprehending form, to that eye consciousness apprehending form, there is an
appearance of external form. That appearance of external form is valid.
Just as the appearance of the external form through the eye consciousness
apprehending it, in reality that is how form exists. There is external
form. According to the Proponent of Sutra, that appearance of external form
is valid.
According to the MOS, an appearance of the external form is not the
representation of how form actually exists. External form does not exist
although it appears. Why there is an appearance of an external form?
According to the MOS, the appearance of the external form is due to the
latencies/ imprints of ignorance that are in the mind, which is due to the
thorough involvement of the mind with ignorance for very very long time.
Back to the Proponent of Sutra on the example of eye consciousness
apprehending form, an eye consciousness apprehending form induces the
conceptual thought thinking; “This is form.”
According to the Proponent of Sutra, there is an external form and this
external form exists as a natural base of engagement /referent of the term
‘form’ from the side of the object.
Now we are talking about Proponent of Sutra.
 • There is an eye consciousness apprehending form.
 • Then it induces the conceptual thought thinking “This is form.” It
   labels “This is form”; not something else.
Through the conceptual consciousness which think “This is form”, there is
an appearance of that external form. There is a form. That form appears as
a natural base of engagement/referent of the term ‘form’.
According to the Proponent of Sutra, not only there is an appearance of
form which exists as a natural base of engagement/referent of  the term
‘form,’  in fact this is how form exist. It exists like that from its own
side as a natural base of engagement/referent for the term ‘form’.
MOS refute this. Although form appears from its own side as a natural base
of engagement/referent of the term ‘form’, in reality that is incorrect and
not valid.
Form does not exist from its own side as the natural base of engagement of
the term ‘form’, rather form existing as the natural referent for the term
‘form’ is merely imputed.
We will attempt to clarify this in the near future, perhaps in the next
class.
The SS asserts external objects and there are objects that are not in the
nature of mind.
The MOS differ greatly in that as they assert everything is in the nature
of mind. How do phenomena exist? Phenomena arise due to the awakening of
karmic propensities or seeds of karma that were infused in the mind.

Now going back to the GES, according to the GES, they say that sense
consciousness, for example, engages its object nakedly without something
intervening between the object and the consciousness perceiving it. That is
what we called an aspect.
In short, the GES do not assert that the consciousnesses are generated in
the aspect of the objects. According to the GES, the sense consciousness
apprehends its object nakedly without intervening aspect.
The object and the subject perceiving it exist simultaneously but these two
are of different substances. So the GES do assert simultaneous cause and
effect.
With the exception of GES, SS and tenets above it assert the
consciousnesses are generated in the aspect of the object it apprehends.
In short, with the exception of GES, all the others Buddhist tenets assert
that consciousnesses are aspected.
We have talked about this in the previous module. Consciousnesses are
aspected. For example, an eye consciousness apprehending blue, an eye
consciousness apprehending blue is generated in the aspect of blue.
In the SS, this aspect is cast from the side of the object. In the case of
blue, this aspect of blue is cast from the object blue. Then the
consciousness apprehending it is generated in the aspect of blue. This is
according to the SS.
The eye consciousness apprehending blue is generated in dependence on blue.
So blue has to exist prior to the production of the eye consciousness
apprehending blue.
➢ In dependence of blue, from the side of blue, an aspect of blue is cast.


➢ Then the eye consciousness apprehending blue is generated.
You can see that the object must exist prior to the subject apprehending
it.
We said that blue appear clearly to the eye consciousness apprehending it.
The reason why we say the blue appears clearly to the eye consciousness
apprehending it because the aspect of blue is appearing clearly to the eye
consciousness apprehending it.
I wonder, in the SS, whether eye consciousness apprehending blue and the
aspect of blue are one substance or not. But it is clear that blue and eye
consciousness apprehending blue are different substances and they don’t
exist simultaneously.  The blue exist prior to the eye consciousness
apprehending it.

The MOS assert that consciousnesses are aspected. For the case of an eye
consciousness apprehending blue, this eye consciousness apprehending blue

that this aspect is cast from the side of the object.

generated in the aspect of blue, but this aspect of blue is not cast from
the side of the object blue. Rather the aspect of blue is generated in
dependence on the awakening of karmic seeds/ propensities.

Because this factor of appearance of blue as blue is appearing as an

If you understand this perhaps you can see why, according to the False
Aspectarians, the appearance of blue as blue is considered to be mistaken.
For the False Aspectarians, when the appearance of blue as blue appears, it
appears an external blue.
For the True Aspectarians, in general, when blue appears, blue appears as
external object. But to the sense direct perceiver apprehending blue, the
appearance of blue as blue does not appear as external blue. Therefore for
them the appearance of blue as blue is not mistaken.
The points you have to get are:
➢ why the True Aspectarians say that the factor of appearance of blue as
  blue is unmistaken
➢ whereas the False Aspectarians says that is mistaken.

True Aspectarians, the factor of appearance of blue as blue does not appear
as external blue. Therefore, the factor of appearance of blue as blue is
not mistaken.
For the False Aspectarians, there is an appearance of external blue and
that is mistaken. But for them, even the appearance of blue as blue is
mistaken because the appearance of blue as blue appears as external blue.

(Root text page 14)
There are three types of True Aspectarians:
1. Proponents of an Equal Number of Apprehendeds and Apprehenders,*1
2. Half-Eggists, and
3. Non-Pluralists.

Each has their own individual assertions.
It is said that the Proponents of an Equal Number of Apprehendeds and
Apprehenders assert that when an eye consciousness apprehending the mottled
colors on the wing of a butterfly apprehends the mottle, from the object’s
side the aspect of each different color – blue, yellow, and so on – is
delivered*2, and also from the subject’s side the aspect of each different
color – blue, yellow, and so on – is produced in its true aspect.
It is said that the Half-Eggists assert that when such is apprehended, from
the object’s side the aspect of each different color – blue, yellow, and so
on – is delivered, however from the subject’s side the aspect of each
different color – blue, yellow, and so on – is produced in an aspectless
manner.
It is said that the Non-Pluralists assert that when such is apprehended,
from the object’s side the aspect of each different color – blue, yellow,
and so on – is not delivered, but rather the aspect of the mere
conglomeration is delivered; and from the subject’s side the aspect of each
different color – blue, yellow, and so on – is not produced in an
aspectless manner, but rather the aspect of the mere conglomeration is
produced in an aspectless manner.

- From the side of the object, the aspects of those various colors are not

- From the side of the subject, the consciousness is generated in the

the various colors that make up the mottle.
In that sense, it is half-half.
Simply speaking, if you look at the butterfly, you see the mottled colors
on the wing. So the aspect of the mere mottle is cast. Then the
consciousness apprehending the mere mottle is generated.
This is one of the rare occasions that we go against the root text. Of

Whereas the Proponents of an Equal Number of Apprehendeds and Apprehenders,

From the side of the object, here the mottle on the wings of the butterfly,
