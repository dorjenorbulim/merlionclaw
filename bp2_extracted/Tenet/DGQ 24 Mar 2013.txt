PRESENTATION OF TENETS

Questions for discussion on Sunday, 24th March 2013



1. (a) Is there a difference between a Buddhist and a proponent of Buddhist
        tenets?

  (b) Does a proponent of Buddhist tenets necessarily accept the four
seals?



2. (a) What is the difference between the two truths in the Great
     Exposition School?

  (b) If it is an ultimate truth, is it necessarily partless?



3. Is there a difference in the mode of apprehension of the coarse and
  subtle apprehension of the self of persons?
