Amitabha Buddhist Centre                                                                Second Basic Program – Module 6  
                                                                                                                                                Tathagata Essence    
 
                                                                                                                                                            
 
DISCUSSION QUESTIONS FOR SUNDAY, 26 APRIL 2015  
 
 
1. Discuss the meaning of Verse 27.  
 
 
2. Verse 25 lists the four points that are difficult to comprehend. What are they?  
 
 
3. With reference to Verse 32, what are the four obscurations and what are their 
antidotes?  
 
 
4. Explain the connection between the four antidotes and the four results , which are 
the perfection of the qualities of purity, self, bless and permanence listed in Verse 
34. (Verses 36 & 37 are also related to this question).  
 
 
Optional questions:  
 
 
5. Discuss  the nine examples (listed in Verse 97 & 98) and the meanings that they 
exemplify.  
 
 
6. There are twelve links of dependent origination related to the class of thoroughly 
afflicted phenomena  but there are also twelve links of dependent origination that 
are r elated to the class of thoroughly pure phenomena. If so, what do you think 
they are?
