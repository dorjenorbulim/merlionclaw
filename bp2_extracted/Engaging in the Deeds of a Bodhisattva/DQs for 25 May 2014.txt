DISCUSSION QUESTIONS FOR SUNDAY, 25TH MAY 2014

1.  What  is  the  difference  between  mindfulness  and  introspection  (or
vigilance)?


2. (a) What are the reasons for guarding the mind?

     (b) What are the benefits of guarding the mind?


3.  What are the faults and what problems will  there  be  when  we  do  not
protect the mind?

4.  It is said that when we do not possess  introspection,  our  ethics  (or
ethical discipline) will not be pure. Why is this so?


5.  How is introspection generated in dependence upon mindfulness?


6.  Explain the exceptions mentioned in Verse 5.42.
