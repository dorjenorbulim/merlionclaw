DISCUSSION QUESTIONS FOR SUNDAY, 27TH APRIL 2014



1. (a)      What is the manner of rejoicing?

     (b)    How does one accumulate merit when rejoicing in the virtue of
others?



2.    The Buddha has conquered the four maras including the mara of death.
The Buddha is also omniscient and knows the wishes and dispositions of all
sentient beings. What then is the purpose of requesting the Buddha not to
pass into parinirvana and to turn the wheel of Dharma?



3.    It is said that conventional bodhicitta destroys both the afflictive
obscurations and the knowledge obscurations. How is this done?



4. (a)      After generating bodhicitta, why is it inappropriate to give up
bodhicitta?

     (b)   What happens when one gives up the bodhicitta that has been
generated?



5.  (a)     What are the faults of the afflictions?

      (b)  When they arise, how are we harmed by them?
