A dream elephant is empty of elephant. Dream elephant is not elephant. So there is a dream elephant and its emptiness of elephant. The dream elephant is not separate from its emptiness of elephant. 
Form and form’s emptiness of existing inherently are not the same; they are different. Although they are not the same thing, they are one entity. Although they are one entity, they are not the same.
