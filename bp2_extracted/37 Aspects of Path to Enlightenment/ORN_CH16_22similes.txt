The 22 Similes of Convention al Mahayana Generated Mind 
 
 COMPANION SIMILE CONCORDANT PROPERTY BOUNDARIES 
1 Aspiration  The earth Being a property that is the basis of all 
wholesome qualities Small path of 
accumulation  
2 Attitude Fine gold Unchanging until enlightenment Middle path of 
accumulation 
3 Exceptional resolve The new moon Fully increasing all virtuous qualities Great path of 
accumulation 
4 Application Fire Burning the firewood of obscuration to 
the three exalted knowers of all Path of 
preparation  
5 Generosity A great treasure Satisfying all sentient beings 1st ground 
6 Ethics A jewel-mine Being a property that is the support of 
precious excellent qualities 2nd ground 
7 Patience A great ocean Being undisturbed by the descending of 
all undesirables 3rd ground 
8 Joyous effort A vajra Through firm conviction being 
inseparable 4th ground 
9 Concentration The king of 
mountains Being unmoved by distracting objects 5th ground 
10 Wisdom Medicine Thoroughly pacifying the diseases of 
afflictive and knowledge obscurations 6th ground 
11 Skillful means A virtuous 
spiritual guide Not forsaking the welfare sentient beings 
on any occasions 7th ground 
12 Prayer A wish-granting 
gem  Accomplishing the results of wishes 
accordingly 8th ground 
13 Power The sun Fully ripening trainees 9th ground 
14 The perfection of 
exalted wisdom A pleasant 
Dharma melody Teaching the Dharma that makes 
trainees aspire 10thground 
15 Clairvoyance A great king Accomplishing others’ welfare through 
unobstructed might 3 pure grounds 
16 Merit and exalted 
wisdom A treasury Being like a treasury of many collections 
of merit and exalted wisdom 3 pure grounds 
17 Dharmas in harmony 
with the class of 
enlightenment A highway Being that which all aryas traveled and 
will travel 3 pure grounds 
18 Compassion and 
special insight A mount Through not falling into either samsara 
or nirvana, progressing easily 3 pure grounds 
19 Retention  and 
confidence A geyser Through retaining heard and unheard 
dharmas, inexhaustible 3 pure grounds 
20 A festival of Dharma A pleasant sound Pleasantly proclaiming to trainees who 
desire liberation Preparation of 
buddha ground 
21 The one path traveled The flow of a 
river Activities of others’ welfare being not 
different Actual of buddha 
ground

in the abode of Tushita and so forth Conclusion of 
buddha ground
