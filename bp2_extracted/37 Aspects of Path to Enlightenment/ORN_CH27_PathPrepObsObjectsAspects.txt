Masters Program 2008: Ornament  – Chart 27 
Objects of Observation and Aspects of  
the Mahayana Path of Preparation 
 Level Objects of Observation Aspects 
small The 16 bearing the support of the truths, 
impermanence and so forth. 
 
 
“The objects of observation are impermanence and so 
forth, 
Bearing the support of the truths .” [1.28ab] The exalted wisdom of the small heat level 
which stops strong adherence, exaggerated 
abidance and so forth with respect to the 16 
attributes of those 4 truths. 
 
“The aspect is stopping strong adherence 
of that and so forth.” 
[1.28c] middling The 4 truths distinguished by freedom from truly 
existent assembly and establishment of forms and so 
forth. 
 
 
“Freedom from assembly and establishment of forms 
and so forth,…” [1.29a] The exalted wisdom of the middling heat 
level realizing freedom from abidance of 
the continuity of name [ultimately] and the 
lack of ultimate abidance. 
 
“... abidance and  
Abidance,…”   [1.29ab]  HEAT 
great  Forms and so forth distinguished by imputation of 
terminology. 
 
“...imputation…”  [1.29b] The exalted wisdom of the great heat level 
realizing the lack of expression of those 
ultimately. 
 
“…and the lack of expression.”  [1.29b] 
 
 Level Objects of Observation Aspects 
small  1. 4 truths not having been presented :  
Forms and so forth that do not abide ultimately, 
distinguished by their entity lacking nature ultimately. 
 
 
“Those forms and so forth which do not abide, 
Their entity lacking nature.”  [1.29cd] 
 
2. 4 truths having been presented :  
Forms and so forth distinguished by not abiding as 
impermanent and so forth ultimately, due to being 
empty of those entities. 
 
“They do not abide as impermanent and so forth; 
Those are empty of those entities.”  [1.30bc] 1. 4 truths not having been presented :  
The exalted wisdom of the small peak level 
realizing that they – forms and so forth and 
their dharmata – are the nature of one 
another. 
 
“They are the nature of one another.”  
[1.30a] 
 
2. 4 truths having been presented :  
The exalted wisdom of the small peak level 
realizing that they – impermanence and so 
forth and their dharmata – are the nature of 
one another. 
 
“They are the nature of one another.”  
[1.30d] middling Whatever phenomena not apprehended as signs 
[ultimately]. 
 
“Whatever phenomena not apprehended,”  [1.31a] The exalted wisdom of the middling peak 
level realizing that they are not seen as 
signs of true existence. 
 
“Because they are not seen as signs,”  
[1.31b] PEAK 
great  The 4 truths distinguished by fully investigating and 
having investigated with the wisdom analyzing the 
ultimate. 
 
“Fully investigating with wisdom,”  [1.31c] The exalted wisdom of the great peak level 
which realizes that all the three circles are 
not observed to be truly existent.  
 
“All are not observed.”  [1.31d]

forbearance level realizing that, due to not 
being based upon their signs ultimately, 
does not imagine and realizes the lack of 
discrimination with the two attentions.

Does not imagine and lack of
