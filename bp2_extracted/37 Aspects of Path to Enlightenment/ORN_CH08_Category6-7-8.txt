CATEGORY 7: Instantaneous Application (U+-%Ü#-07Ü-‚ë9-/-)– 4 Topics 
# Topic 
Definiendum Definition Boundaries 
1 Non-ripened 
instantaneous application  A bodhisattva’s yoga that serves as a direct antidote to 
the knowledge obstructions by means of being conjoined with a wisdom such that, when one uncontaminated non-ripened quality is actualized, those concordant in type with it are also actualized in the shortest instant in which an action is completed. 
 
2 Ripened 
instantaneous application A bodhisattva’s yoga that serves as a direct antidote to 
the knowledge obstructions by means of being conjoined with a wisdom such that, when one uncontaminated ripened quality is actualized, those concordant in type with it are also actualized in the shortest instant in which an action is completed. 
 
3 Characterless 
instantaneous application A bodhisattva’s yoga that serves as a direct antidote to 
the knowledge obstructions by means of being conjoined with the wisdom directly realizing emptiness. 
 
4 Non-dual 
instantaneous application A bodhisattva’s yoga that serves as a direct antidote to 
the knowledge obstructions by means of being conjoined with the wisdom directly realizing the emptiness of apprehended and apprehender being different substances. 
 exists only at the end of continuum [of a sentient being]

# Topic 
Definiendum Definition Boundaries 
1 Nature body A final sphere possessing the two purities.  
2 Complete 
enjoyment body  A final form body possessing the five certainties.  
[The five certainties are: 1. certainty of abode: it resides only in Akanishta (Not Low) 2. certainty of body: it is adorned with the clear and complete signs and exemplifications 3. certainty of retinue: it is surrounded only by bodhisattva aryas 4. certainty of Dharma: it teaches only the Mahayana Dharma 5. certainty of time: it remains as long as cyclic existence is not empty] 
3 Emanation 
body A final form body distinguished by not possessing the 
five certainties.  
4 Exalted 
wisdom truth body  An exalted knower that is a final insight relative to 
modes and varieties.
 exists only on the buddha ground 
 
  
Exalted 
activity of an exalted wisdom truth body A very positive
 excellent quality arisen from an exalted 
wisdom truth body (that acted as the empowering condition) which is its cause.  exists from not
