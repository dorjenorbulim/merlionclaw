Masters Program 2008: Ornament  – Chart 3 
 CATEGORY 1: Exalted Knower of All Aspects (F0-0aè,-) – Ten Topics 
# Topic 
Definiendum Definition Boundaries 
1 Mahayana 
conventional generated mind A mahayana special main mental knower 
that arises concomitant with an assisting aspiration observing complete enlightenment for others’ welfare and is distinguished by abiding in a class of paths that serves as the door of entry into mahayana paths. exists from the mahayana 
path of accumulation through the buddha ground 
2 Mahayana 
instructions  Mahayana speech that teaches a method for 
attaining the object of striving of the mahayana generated mind.  exists from not having 
entered a path through the buddha ground   
3 Mahayana path 
of preparation 
(mutually 
inclusive with “mahayana partial concordance with definite discrimination”)  A mahayana mundane path that abides in a 
class of clear realizations that arise subsequent to the completion of the partial concordance with liberation and is concordant with a clear realization of the truth.  exists only on the mahayana 
path of preparation  
4 Lineage abiding 
in the nature, the support of mahayana achievings The common locus of that which is a 
dharmata in the continuum of a bodhisattva and that which acts as an actual supporting basis of mahayana achievings.  exists from the path of 
accumulation through the end of the continuum [of a sentient being] 
5 Object of 
observation of mahayana achievings  
(mutually 
inclusive with “object of knowledge”) A basis of elimination of superimpositions 
by a mahayana achieving.  exists on whatever is an 
established base 
6 Object of intent 
of mahayana achievings 
(mutually 
inclusive with “buddha”)  The final result that is the object of intent for 
which one engages.  exists only on the buddha 
ground

7 Achieving of 
armor 
(mutually 
inclusive with “a bodhisattva’s exalted knower”) A bodhisattva’s yoga conjoined with an 
extensive attitude desiring to practice inclusively the six perfections, generosity and so forth, in their entirety within each of the six perfections.  exists from the mahayana 
path of accumulation through the end of the continuum [of a sentient being] 
8 Achieving of 
engagement  A bodhisattva’s yoga practicing an 
application in some type of quality of mahayana cause and effect by means of mainly joyous effort.  exists from the heat level of 
the mahayana path of preparation through the end of the continuum [of a sentient being] 
9 Achieving of 
accumulation  A bodhisattva’s yoga that surpasses the 
middling level of supreme mundane quality of the Mahayana path of preparation and below by way of being conjoined with the two extensive accumulations and issues forth great enlightenment as its result.  exists from the great level 
of the supreme mundane quality of the mahayana path of preparation through the end of the continuum [of a sentient being] 
10 Achieving of 
definite issuance A yoga of the pure grounds that undoubtedly 
issues forth an exalted knower of all aspects. exists on the three pure 
grounds
