Masters Program 2008: Ornament  – Chart 4 
CATEGORY 2: Knower of Paths (:0-;è<-) – Eleven Topics  
 Topic 
Definiendum Definition Boundaries 
1 Branches of a  
knower of paths A special excellent quality conjoined 
with great compassion that is included in either the cause, entity, or result of a knower of paths.  exists from having 
awakened the mahayana lineage through the buddha ground 
2 Knower of paths 
knowing hearers’ paths 
(Mutually inclusive 
with “a mahayana arya’s exalted knower abiding in a class of hearers’ realizations”) A mahayana arya’s exalted knower that – 
by means of being conjoined with the 
three, the generated mind, dedication, 
and the wisdom realizing emptiness – 
abides in a class of clear realizations of 
what is to be known in order to lead trainees who are hearer lineage-bearers.  exists from the 
mahayana path of seeing through the buddha ground 
3 Knower of paths 
knowing solitary  realizers’ paths 
(Mutually inclusive 
with “a mahayana arya’s exalted knower  
abiding in a class of solitary realizers’ re-alizations”)  A mahayana arya’s exalted knower that – 
by means of being conjoined with the 
three attributes – abides in a class of 
clear realizations of what is to be known 
in order to lead trainees who are solitary realizer lineage-bearers.  exists from the 
mahayana path of seeing through the buddha ground 
4 Mahayana path  
of seeing A mahayana clear realization of truth 
conjoined with the wisdom directly realizing emptiness in the continuum of the person who possesses it in his continuum.  exists only on the 
mahayana path of seeing
5 Function of a  
mahayana path of  meditation A benefit attained through the force of 
cultivating the mahayana path of meditation which is the method for attaining it.  exists from the second 
instant of cultivating the mahayana path of meditation through the buddha ground 
6 Mahayana path of 
meditation of belief A mahayana subsequent clear realization 
convinced that the Mothers are the source of the three welfares.  exists from the first 
ground through the end of the continuum [of a sentient being] 
7 Benefits of a  
mahayana path of  meditation of belief An excellent quality attained through the 
force of cultivating the path of meditation of belief which is the method for attaining it.  exists from the second 
instant of cultivating the path of meditation of belief through the buddha ground

8 Path of meditation 
of dedication A conceptual mahayana subsequent clear 
realization – apprehending sound 
generalities and meaning generalities as 
suitable to be mixed – that acts to 
transform either one’s own or others’ 
roots of virtue into a branch of complete enlightenment.  exists from the first 
ground through the end of the continuum [of a sentient being] 
9 Path of meditation 
of rejoicing A conceptual mahayana subsequent clear 
realization – apprehending sound 
generalities and meaning generalities as 
suitable to be mixed – that cultivates joy 
in either one’s own or others’ roots of 
virtue.  exists from the first 
ground through the end of the continuum [of a sentient being] 
10 Path of meditation 
of achieving 
(mutually inclusive 
with “a path of meditation  of purity”) An uncontaminated mahayana 
subsequent clear realization that deposits an imprint for the final realization which is its object of attainment.  exists from the first 
ground through the tenth ground 
11 Path of meditation 
of purity An uncontaminated mahayana 
subsequent clear realization that deposits an imprint for the final abandonment which is its object of attainment.  exists from the first 
ground through the tenth ground
