Exalted knower of aspects is a fully developed exalted wisdom that directly
realizing the 10 topics, mind generation and so forth.
What is its boundary? Where does it exist? It exists only on Buddha ground.

There are 10 topics that illustrate this exalted knower of aspects. As for
the manner of how these 10 topics illustrate the exalted knower of aspects,

    - they illustrate the result of the cause and
    - they illustrate the subject - the mind, the knower, the wisdom.
These 10 topics illustrate the exalted knower of aspects. As I have
mentioned, there are two ways of illustration:
- through the avenue of the cause, illustrating that then one comes to know
a result.
- through knowing an object, illustrating that then one comes to know the
subject that realizes it.
Simply speaking, an exalted knower of aspects is the exalted knower in the
continuum of a Buddha superior. It is a consciousness that is forever
separated and free of all the defilements. It is the wisdom truth body.
However, if you look at the definition, you might be wondering what is it
talking about. Simply speaking, it refers to that consciousness (mind) that
is free and is not accompanied by any of the defilements or obscurations.
The definition says “It is a fully developed exalted wisdom directly
realizing the 10 topics, mind generation and so forth.” Through using the
examples of objects like mind generation and so forth, it is trying to show
us what this exalted knower of aspects is. It is a fully developed wisdom
that realizes these objects here, which are the 10 topics. So we are trying
to show what this exalted knower of aspects is via using these 10 topics or
objects to illustrate and helping us understand what an exalted knower of
aspects is.
Having said the definition of what an exalted knower of aspects is, and if
somebody were to ask you:
Is a wisdom that directly realizes mind generation necessarily an exalted
knower of aspects?
Then what would you say?
