# Topic 
Definiendum Definition Boundaries 
1 Peak 
application of heat A first mahayana partial concordance with 
definite discrimination that has attained any of the 12 signs of having attained peak application.  exists only on the heat 
level of the mahayana path of preparation 
2 Peak 
application of peak A second mahayana partial concordance with 
definite discrimination that has attained the 16 thorough increases of merit, such as that exceeding the worshipping of buddhas equal in number to the sentient beings of the billionfold world systems.  exists only on the peak 
level of the mahayana path of preparation 
3 Peak 
application of forbearance A third mahayana partial concordance with 
definite discrimination that has attained stability in (i) the wisdom that is a complete similitude of the three exalted knowers and (ii) indivisibility from the others’ welfare.  exists only on the 
forbearance level of the mahayana path of preparation 
4 Peak 
application of supreme mundane quality A fourth partial concordance with definite 
discrimination that has attained an always abiding mind with respect to infinite meditative stabilizations, on account of having ripened the potency to generate the mahayana path of seeing which is its result. exists only on the 
supreme mundane quality level of the mahayana path of preparation 
5 Peak 
application of the path of seeing A mahayana clear realization of the truth 
abiding in a class of direct antidote to the seeds of the conceptions that are objects to be abandoned by the path of seeing.  exists only on the 
mahayana path of seeing 
6 Peak 
application of the path of meditation A mahayana subsequent clear realization 
abiding in a class of direct antidote to the seeds of the conceptions that are objects to be abandoned by the path of meditation.  exists only on the 
[mahayana] path of meditation 
7 Uninterrupted 
peak application 
(Mutually 
inclusive with “an exalted wisdom at the end of the continuum [of a sentient being]”) A bodhisattva’s final yoga – conjoined with a 
wisdom highly transformed from the mahayana 
path of accumulation that cultivates the aspects of the three exalted knowers through gathering 
them together – that acts to directly produce an 
exalted knower of all aspects which is its result. [exists at the end of the 
continuum of a sentient being] 
8 Perverse 
achieving that is to be eliminated indicated here That which is included in either a seed of a 
conception or a manifest conception apprehending the two truths as not suitable to be subsumed as a single entity indicated here.  exists from not having 
entered a path through the seventh ground
