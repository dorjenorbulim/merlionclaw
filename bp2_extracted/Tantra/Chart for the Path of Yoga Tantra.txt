Essence of approximation:
1) Generates supporting & supported mandalas
2) Generates clear appearance and divine pride
3) Accumulates repetitions of deity’s mantra
4) Concludes with compensation burnt offering ritual
Types of approximation:
1) Time – deciding on length of approximation & completing it
2) Number – accumulate numbers of mantra of the deity 
3) Signs – doing approximation until receiving such signs as seeing deity speaking, etc.
4) Mental stability – achieves the stable yoga of non-dual profundity and clarity
