When you understand the dependent origination, the ignorance does not arise in one’s mind. Therefore, put all effort to understand dependent origination. Something like that. I couldn’t find the exact word in Lama Tsongkhapa’s text.
There are some verses said by Lama Tsongkhapa: “When you follow the Buddha’s teaching, you are able to eliminate all the problems, and also able to eliminate the root of the problem. If you follow other than the Buddha’s teaching, then no matter how you practice, how long you practise, and whatever hardship you put, you are just calling all the problems to come because you are making your self grasping stronger”. 
Je Tsongkhapa’s In Praise of Dependent Origination 
Verse 31
You who speak excellently by seeing as it is,
For those who train in your footsteps,
All degenerations will become remote;
For the root of all faults will be undone.
Verse 32
But those who turn away from your teaching,
Though they may struggle with hardship for a long time,
Faults increase ever more as if being called forth;
For they make firm the view of self.
The point is that if you follow other than Buddha’s teaching, then however you practise, you are making your self grasping stronger. Therefore, Lama Tsongkhapa is saying that even if you don’t want problem, you are just creating the problem. Then by following the Buddha’s teaching, you are able to part off all the problems. I mean cut the root of the problem. Therefore, understanding dependent origination is extremely important. That’s why Lama Tsongkhapa compose the text because he see how extremely important the Buddha is. That is the kind of teaching he give, especially dependent origination. Lama Tsongkhapa put so much devotion to the Buddha. He only see the Buddha’s teaching is able to cut the root of Samsara; not any other teaching,. So he developed so much faith to Buddha.
When you meditate on tantra, emptiness is extremely important. At the very beginning to understand what is emptiness. Last time we talked about the self generation. In order to do self generation, you must have good understanding of emptiness.
