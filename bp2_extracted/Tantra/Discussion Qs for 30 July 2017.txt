QUESTIONS FOR DISCUSSION ON SUNDAY, 30 JULY 2017

   1. (a) It’s said that the four classes of Buddhist tantra  are  different
      in the manner in which desire is taken as the path. How is that so?

      (b) What’s the meaning of “taking desire as the path”?

   2. How does one meditate on the four complete  purities  concordant  with
      the results?

   3. Please describe the process of the absorption with recitation and  the
      absorption not reliant on recitation.


   4. What are the  four  external  branches  of  recitation  and  the  four
      internal branches of recitation in Performance Tantra?


   5. (a) Please provide the citations from Mantric Stages that explain  the
      need for method and wisdom to be found in one consciousness.


      (b)  How does this mind where method and wisdom are fused  become  the
      cause for the dharmakaya and the rupakaya?


   6. (a) In tantra, is the wisdom directly realizing emptiness the yoga  of
      non-dual profundity and clarity?


      (b) If yes, how does this mind act as the substantial  cause  for  the
   form body?


   7. What are:
    • the four seals that are the bases of purification
    • the four seals of the purifying path
    • the four seals of the result  In Yoga Tantra?

   8. What is the yoga focussing on specific hand symbols in Yoga Tantra?

   9. When is calm abiding achieved in Yoga Tantra?


(Khenrinpoche  indicated  questions  were  set  not  only  for  purposes  of
discussion but to provide the opportunity for  revision  and  to  facilitate
familiarity with the material. In the course of preparing answers for  these
questions, you will be able to find out what you don’t  understand  and  any
qualms you may have. These become the basis for further discussion).

Terminology used follows what appears in the root text.
