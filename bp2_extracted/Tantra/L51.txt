Homework
Khen Rinpoche: I want to give the homework. I think the group leader will send them to you. I just want to tell you now. This is part of the Basic Program requirement. Those who really want to take the final exam then must do this (homework). 
I want you to do Lamrim meditation during November, December, and January i.e. for three months. In one month normally we have eight classes i.e. on every Tuesday, and Thursday. So every Tuesday and Thursday in November, December and January (for three months) I want you to do Lamrim meditation. Instead of coming class, you discipline yourself by sitting down on your cushion at 7.30 pm and then start your meditation. 
First choice: I want you to do 15 minutes guru yoga either Six Session Guru Yoga or Lama Tsongkhapa Guru Yoga. It is up to you. 
Then, you meditate Lamrim for 45 minutes. 
So you have one hour time of meditation. 
So if you do the meditation on Tuesday and Thursday every week, so for three months you will do meditation for 24 hours. Each month you do the meditation for 8 hours. So November you do meditation for 8 hours, December you do meditation for eight hours, and January you do meditation for eight hours. So total you do meditation for 24 hours. 
So if you do the meditation on every Tuesday and Thursday for three months, then you get 100 marks. This mark you have to give it to yourself; nobody else is giving. You have to be honest with yourself. So if you miss one session, therefore you do only for 23 hours instead of 24 hours, then you deduct 5 marks. If you miss two sessions, you deduct 10 marks.
Second choice: I want you to do same thing but the time is 45 minutes; which is lesser. This is your choice. 
For 15 minutes you do guru yoga and you do 30 minutes Lamrim meditation. 
This is second choice. If you want 100 marks, do the meditation for 1 hour. Here you do 45 minutes meditation, you get 75 marks. Same thing, if you miss one session of the 45 minutes meditation, deduct 5 marks. 
Third choice: Another choice is to do the meditation for 30 minutes: 15 minutes guru yoga and 15 minutes Lamrim meditation. This choice you get 50 marks. So if you do that that means you just pass.
Fourth choice:  Just do 15 minutes Lamrim meditation. If you do that for three months on every Tuesday and Thursday, you will get 25 marks. 
You have to choose either doing the meditation one hour, 45 minutes, 30 minutes or 15 minutes. You have to be honest. When we start our class again, you give me your mark; whether 100 marks or 75 marks are up to you. Write down the mark and pass back to me. Write down your name and states “I have 100 marks” or “I have 75 marks”. 
This is the homework. Try for three months. Those who really seriously want to take final exam and also those who really seriously want to get enlighten. 
You can choose any day to do the meditation. It is up to you. I am just giving the example of Tuesday and Thursday because of the class. You can do it on Wednesday. It is up to you. 
You have to do the meditation in one sitting. I am not expecting a good meditation. I want you to try your best. Put some effort and try. So that is my point. 
Just concentrate on the small and middle scope of the Lamrim. Keep the Great Scope aside. 
(For the Lamrim retreat) We supposed to do for two weeks. That one I will think about it because I didn’t have the time and also I didn’t get the place. The other option is what I am giving you now. I will see whether that can be covered two weeks meditation. I have to think about that. 
When I said guru yoga, for example Lama Tsongkhapa Guru Yoga, first visualize and then establish the seven limbs practice according to the sequence. As for Six Session Guru Yoga, I have explained that day - visualize extensive offering is there and do the seven limbs prayer. 
Be honest with yourself and give your own mark. That makes it very easy so nobody needs to mark and you mark yourself. You want to give 100 marks or 50 marks - it is up to you. Nobody can judge. Only you can. At least try. I have given you different choices.
