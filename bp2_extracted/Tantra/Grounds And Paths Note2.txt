The stages of leaving the repetition performed in dependence on the four branches:
1. Observation of the sound of the mantra
2. Form of the letters of the mantra on the moon in the heart of the deity in front
3. The moon in the heart of the deity in front
4. The body of the deity in front
5. One’s own divine body
6. The form of the letters standing on the moon disc at one’s heart
7. The sound of the mantra
8. Wisdom body
9. Form body
- The path of Action tantra is subsumed by three forms of meditation:
	1. Meditation on the deity’s body – Mahamudra
Meditation on the six deities
	2. Meditation on the deity’s speech – Mantra
Concentration with repetition:
		– Repetition observing the form of the letters
		– Repetition observing on the sound of the mantra
		Concentration without repetition:
		– Concentration abiding in fire
		– Concentration of abiding in sound
	3. Meditating on the deity’s mind – Suchness
		Concentration without repetition:
		– Concentration bestowing liberation at the end of sound
