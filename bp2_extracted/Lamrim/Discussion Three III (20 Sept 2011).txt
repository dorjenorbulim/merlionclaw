How to assert the existence of the lower realms Lower Realms:
- Does the lower realms exist? Do the hell realms exist?
- If yes, how do you prove hell realms and lower realms exist?
- How can beings exist in the lower realms? How is that possible?

If we have to take rebirth due to afflictions and karma, it is in any of
the three realms:
   1. Desire realm   2. Form realm    3. Formless realm
Desire realm can be divided into:
1. Happy realms (God, demigod, human)
2. Miserable realms (animals, hungry ghost, hell beings)
Whatever realms one takes rebirth in depends on the positive or negative
karma that one has accumulated.
We can see human and animal realms. What about hungry ghost and hell
realms?
Human realm – why humans have different levels of happiness?
-Higher rebirth with opportunity to practice dharma.
-Lower rebirth with miserable mentality that is cruel and selfish and has
no interest in dharma.
-Some have great suffering due to poverty, misfortune, unattractive. Some
born in the midst of famine or war.
God- as the result of the fruits of previously created karma that was not
dedicated to the goals of liberation and enlightenment. With heavenly
environments where is great and long lasting happiness but end up lower
rebirth after the exhaustion of the positive karma.
Demigod- have pleasurable life but full of jealousy as they can see the
superior conditions of the gods. They constantly complete and struggle with
the gods but they never win due to their karma which cannot match with the
god. This serves to increase the demigod frustration.
Animal –full of ignorance, live in constant fear and preying on other
animals and human beings to stay alive.
Hungry ghost - born in environment of extreme desolation and lack of
resources especially food and water. Obsess with finding the sustenance of
food and drink, they exhaust themselves in constant fruitless searching.
Even if they manage to find it, due to their karma, they are unable to gain
any benefit due to either the food  turn to fire or the drink increase
their thirst. Constantly undergo suffering.
Hell beings- experiencing constant pain and suffering for eons.
Understanding Existence: Types of Phenomena
There are three different kinds of phenomena:
1. Manifest Phenomena: Those are the ones we can contact directly with our
senses. Animals are manifest phenomena, we can see that.
2. Hidden Phenomena: These are things that we understand through inference
such as emptiness or lack of inherent existence as we understand emptiness
initially through logic and reasoning, and only later do we realize it with
direct perception.
3. Extremely Hidden Phenomena: These are things that we come to understand
through accepting somebody else’s word on it because that person is very
knowledgeable and has no reason at all to deceive us.
For the realms of the hungry ghosts or the hell beings, it would be
manifest phenomena for the beings living there. But for us, they are the
extremely hidden phenomena and we have to rely on the word of somebody else
to understand them and then keep checking it out to see if it makes sense
for us.
If you have faith in the Buddha, then you might provisionally accept
existence of lower realms that we can’t see because the Buddha had
described them and there is no reason for him to deceive us.
If you believe that there is Pure Land even though you cannot see it, how
come you do not believe in hell realms?
We can establish the existence of hell realms through the understanding of
karma.
Lesson 21: If you understand the suffering on your  individual  basis,  then
you will be able to  understand  what  others  are  going  through.  We  can
understand the existence of lower realms through logic and reasoning.
-As a human being, we have problems and  under  the  control  of  sufferings
especially when our wishes are not fulfilled and our desires are not met.
-For beings that are lower than us, based on our  own  experience  as  human
being, we can infer the kind of sufferings they are going through.
Animals - ignorant and unable to tell us what they want.
-Although we can’t directly perceive hungry ghosts, we  can  use  our  human
experience  to  infer  their  state  of  existence  and   their   sufferings
especially those are sick, deprive of sustenance.
