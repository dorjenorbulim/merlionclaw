 Note from the editor 
 
 
‘LRCM’   refers to Volume 1, 2 and 3 of the Lamrim Chenmo  translation, published by Snow Lion Publications: 
 
The Great Treatise on the Stages of the Path to Enlightenment , by Tsong-kha-pa. Translated by  the Lamrim Chenmo Translation 
Committee. Ithaca: Snow Lion Publications; Volume 1: 2000, Volume 2: 2004,  and Volume 3: 2002. 
 
 
‘Pabongka’   refers to part one, two and three of Liberation in our Hands: 
 
Liberation in Our Hands (Part One: The Preliminaries; Part Two: The Fundamentals; Part Three: The Ultimate Goals),  by Pabongka 
Rinpoche. Translated by Sera Mey Geshe Lobsang Tharchin with Artemus B. Engle. Howell, New Jersey: Mahayana Sutra 
and Tantra Press, 1990 and 1994. 
 
 
‘Gen Sopa’   refers to Volume 1, 2 and 3 of Steps on the Path to Enlightenment: 
 
Steps on the Path to Enlightenment, Volu me 1: A Commentary on the Lamrim Chenmo, The Foundation al Practices; Volume 2: 
Karma;  Volume 3: The Way of the Bodhisattva , by Geshe Lhundub Sopa . Wisdom Publications.
