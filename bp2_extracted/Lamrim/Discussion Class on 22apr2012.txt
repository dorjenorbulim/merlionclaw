Question 5:
In order to take rebirth in Cyclic Existence, does one need to have the
ignorance that is part of the12 links of dependent arising?
-35:07
KR: It is very clear in the text that even for those who have realized
selfless directly, there still some who still take rebirth in the cyclic
existence under the control of karma and afflictions. Did you see that?
What did it say? The text says that even those who realized selfless
directly, there is still some of them who take rebirth in cyclic existence
under the power of their karma and afflictions. Remember those who have
realize selfless directly become super beings, superior. Superiors do not
accumulate fresh projecting karma right?
VG: Why? No afflictions? They still have afflictions, everything is still
there.
KR: I think forgot? I did mention before.
It’s clear in the text that even those who have realized selfless directly
they still take rebirth in cyclic existence due to karma and afflictions
but they do not accumulate fresh projecting karma.
Why do superior not accumulate fresh projecting karma?
-31.59
You would have to say that because they do not have the ignorance from the
12 links. The superiors have ignorance, those superiors who have not
eradicated ignorance, they have ignorance but they do not have the
ignorance that is of the 12 links. We have to end up saying that because
they do not have the ignorance of the 12 links of dependent arising
therefore they do not accumulate fresh projecting karma.
SO that is the essence of the question. If you have to answer you have to
say that basically to take rebirth in cyclic existence we do not need the
ignorance that is of the 12 links of dependent arising.

Qn: In LRCM page 323, it mentioned that arhats have immeasurable projecting
karma accumulated when they were ordinary beings but are free of cyclic
existence because they do not have afflictions. I.
VG: Ignorance is root of afflictions.
-
VG: Arhats are necessary superior but not all Superiors are not necessary
Arhats.
KR: They are not same. Arhats they do not have any afflictions.

-24:06
Qn: Superiors beings do not have ignorance of the 12 links therefore they
do not accumulate fresh projecting karma. coming back to the stream
enterers and once returners. When you say they do not have ignorance of 12
links, what ignorance..would they
They still have ignorance of the 12 links won’t they? What ignorance would
there be apart from the ignorance of the 12 links?

-22:57
KR: I have said it quite clearly that ignorance is the view of the
transitory collections. And the VIEW OF TRANSITORY COLLECTION is
apprehending the “I” in one’s own continuum to be self sufficient,
substantially existent. In order to be the ignorance of the 12 links of
dependent arising, it is not sufficient for it just to be ignorance, but it
has to be ignorance that has the power to accumulate a projecting karma in
cyclic existence. In order to be ignorance of the12 links, it not just
sufficient for it to be ignorance but has to be ignorance that has the
power, the strength to cause accumulation of projecting karma. The direct
antidote to ignorance is the wisdom realizing selflessness. When one
realizes selflessness directly for the first time, that realization causes
one’s ignorance to be weakened, but ignorance is still not removed
completely.
-19:08 When the person become superior, he or she sees the truth directly
for the first time, ignorance is weakened but he has not destroyed
ignorance yet. Still there is ignorance, anger and attachment but it will
be a weakened form. It will only be destroyed completely when “foe
destroyer hood” is achieved.
Ignorance has a bigger boundary. Ignorance of 12 links of dependent arising
has a smaller boundary, a smaller pervasion. Ignorance in general
encompasses more stuff and ignorance of 12 links of dependent arising is
more specific and encompasses something small.

Question 3:

projecting karma as a desire realm god?

-5:35

to a very powerful object, like the merit field are exception regardless of
the motivation, they are virtuous.
There has to be a difference between these two projecting karmas. These two
karmas are variable karmas, so it doesn’t mean that karma that is
accumulated will automatically ripen in the form a rebirth as a human
being. Yes or no? Variable or not? After you have accumulated the
projecting karma to be born as a human being, do you necessarily have to
experience the result of that karma on the basis of a human body? Yes or
no? Variable karma? The Answer has to be? Not just yes or no, have to give
Why.
