 What is a sense direct perceiver? A sense direct perceiver possesses the
following three characteristics:
   1. that is produced in dependence on its uncommon empowering condition, a
      physical sense power
   2. is non-conceptual (free from conceptuality)
   3. is non-mistaken
This is how you understand a sense direct perceiver. So a sense direct
perceiver is a mind that has these three characteristics.
If someone were to ask: “Is a direct perceiver necessarily a valid
cognizer?”
You should be able to say: “Not necessary so’ but you have to understand
why it is not necessary so.
The notes in the handout (dated 10 July 2012) clearly stated sense direct
perceiver has three divisions:
   1. sense direct perceivers that are valid cognizers
   2. sense direct perceivers that are subsequent cognizers
   3. sense direct perceiver that are awareness to which an object appears
      and is not ascertained (AAA)
From these you should be able to know instantly that if it is a sense
direct perceiver it is not necessary a valid cognizer.
(Definition of valid cognizer: a new incontrovertible knower)
From the chart on the seven fold consciousness, it shows very clearly that
how many are valid cognizers and how many are non-valid awareness. You
should be able to say immediately how many are and how many are not.
