THE STUDY OF MIND AND ITS FUNCTIONS

Questions for discussion on Sunday, 22nd July 2012


1. If it is an established  base,  is  it  necessarily  all  three  of  the
   following: (1) appearing object (2) determined object, and (3) object of
   engagement?


2. Object possessors are divided into: (1) persons (2) awarenesses, and (3)
   expressive sounds.
     a) Why are they object possessors?
     b) If it is a sound, is it necessarily an object possessor?


3. (a)  If an object appears to a direct perceiver, is it  necessarily  its
   appearing object?
     (b)  If  an  object  appears  to  a  conceptual  consciousness,  is  it
      necessarily not its      appearing object?


4. Among the seven-fold division of consciousness, how  many  are  mistaken
   consciousnesses and how many are non-mistaken consciousnesses?


5. (a) What is the uncommon empowering condition, the mental sense power?

   (b) If it is a consciousness that arises in dependence on a mental sense
      power, is it   necessarily a mental direct perceiver?
