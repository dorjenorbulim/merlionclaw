Question 3:
Dharmakirti said “Because it is impermanent, it is miserable, it is
selfless.”
   a) Explain: how it is miserable because it is impermanent, how it is
      selfless because it is miserable.
   b) Give an illustration of cyclic existence.

-48.59
KR: Definition of cyclic existence is that taking rebirth under the power
of karma and afflictions. Taking on body and mind due to the power of karma
and afflictions. Does the body circle from life to life?
Ans: Most subtle wind that circles uncontrollably in cyclic existence is an
illustration of samsara.

KR: Is ignorance samsara or not?
Ans: Yes
KR: So ignorance is samsara. We have to posit a cause for samsara, so cause
of samsara is ignorance ya? Wouldn’t it seems that ignorance has to pre-
ceed samsara? Ignorance acts as a condition then samsara comes as a result.
Isn’t it like that? Is ignorance samsara or not? So ignorance doesn’t go
from life to life?

where you went wrong you will not forget. Otherwise in debate if you don’t

In debate when someone challenges your position, the person’s aim is to
destroy your thesis. You have a thesis you believe in something, the whole
point is to challenge that position. To challenge the position, there’s
many way directly or indirectly coming from various avenues. That’s how we
challenge a position.

In examining whether the apprehension of self of a person or self of a
phenomena is same or not. In regards to the person you can focus on
someone’s aggregates and apprehend that person to be self sufficient
