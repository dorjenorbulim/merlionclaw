## 📋 Executive Summary

**Market Opportunity:** Growing demand for online religious services, accelerated by pandemic, continued by convenience and diaspora needs.

**Key Findings:**
- ✅ Strong demand for online memorial/prayer services
- ✅ Membership models working ($10-88/month tiers)
- ✅ Individual rituals priced $50-2,000+ depending on complexity
- ✅ Hybrid model (online + physical) most successful
- ⚠️ Trust and authenticity critical (fraud concerns in market)

**Recommended Starting Point:** Hybrid membership + à la carte services

## 🛠️ Technology Requirements

### Platform Options

| Option | Cost (Setup) | Cost (Monthly) | Pros | Cons |
|--------|--------------|----------------|------|------|
| **Custom Website + Payment** | $5,000-15,000 | $200-500 | Full control, branding | High upfront cost |
| **WordPress + Plugins** | $2,000-5,000 | $100-300 | Lower cost, flexible | Maintenance required |
| **Buy Me a Coffee / Patreon** | $0 | 5-12% of revenue | Easy start, built-in audience | Limited customization, fees |
| **3D Virtual Temple (like 福慧天开)** | $50,000+ | $2,000+ | Immersive experience | Very high cost, niche |

**Recommended:** Start with WordPress + membership plugin, upgrade later.

### Essential Features

- ✅ User registration/login
- ✅ Payment gateway (PayNow, GrabPay, credit card)
- ✅ Video streaming (Zoom integration or YouTube Live)
- ✅ Prayer/memorial submission forms
- ✅ Member-only content area
- ✅ Donation tracking (tax receipts if registered charity)
- ✅ Multilingual (English, Chinese, optionally Malay)

## ⚠️ Market Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| **Trust/Fraud Concerns** | High | High | - Transparent pricing<br>- Master credentials displayed<br>- Testimonials, reviews<br>- Physical temple address |
| **Competition (Established Temples)** | Medium | Medium | - Niche focus (online-first)<br>- Better UX<br>- Diaspora market (underserved) |
| **Regulatory (Charity Registration)** | Medium | Medium | - Consult lawyer early<br>- Start as social enterprise<br>- Register as charity if scaling |
| **Technology Failure** | Medium | Low | - Reliable hosting<br>- Backup systems<br>- Customer support |
| **Cultural Authenticity** | High | High | - Qualified masters/teachers<br>- Proper lineage<br>- Community advisory board |
