🛡️ Our Ethical Framework

MerlionClaw only showcases use cases that meet these standards:

Transparency

Clear about what the AI does and doesn't do. No hidden automation.

Human Agency

AI assists, not replaces. Humans remain in control of decisions.

Privacy First

Respect personal data. No scraping, no unauthorized access.

Social Good

Enriches lives — financially, emotionally, or practically.

Legal Compliance

Follows Singapore laws (PDPA, copyright, financial regulations).

No Harm

Doesn't deceive, manipulate, or exploit vulnerable people.
