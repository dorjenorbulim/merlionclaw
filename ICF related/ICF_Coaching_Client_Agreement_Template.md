## Section 1: Coaching Relationship

### What is Coaching?
Coaching is a forward-focused, client-driven process that supports you in:
- Clarifying goals and vision
- Developing strategies and action plans
- Building accountability and sustainable change
- Discovering your own solutions through powerful questioning

**Coaching is NOT:**
- Therapy, counseling, or mental health treatment
- Consulting (I won't give you expert advice)
- Mentoring (I won't share my experience as the answer)
- Medical or legal advice

### Coaching Approach
I follow the International Coaching Federation (ICF) Core Competencies and Code of Ethics. Our work together is a partnership — you are the expert in your life, and I am here to support your discovery and growth.

I use AI tools to enhance your coaching experience:

**What AI Does:**
- ✅ Prepares session notes and summaries

## Section 3: Confidentiality

### What Stays Confidential
- Everything shared in our coaching sessions
- Session notes and records
- Goals, plans, and personal information
- Communication between sessions

### Exceptions to Confidentiality
I am required to break confidentiality if:
1. **Imminent Harm:** You express intent to harm yourself or others
2. **Legal Requirement:** Court order or legal mandate
3. **Child/Elder Abuse:** Disclosure of abuse to vulnerable persons
4. **Client Consent:** You specifically authorize disclosure

### AI & Confidentiality
AI tools process data locally and do not share information with external cloud services. This maintains a higher standard of confidentiality than many AI-assisted coaching platforms.
