## What Is AgentHub?

AgentHub is a curated marketplace for AI agent skills — discover, install, and share capabilities that extend what your AI assistant can do. Think of it as an app store for AI agents.

**Browse & Discover:**

**Install & Use:**

- Skills integrate directly into your AI assistant

**Publish Your Own:**

## Getting Started

**To browse:** Visit https://marketplace-5gjkyefp7-agent-where.vercel.app/#developers

**To install skills:**
```bash
clawhub install <skill-name>
```

**To publish your own:**
```bash
clawhub publish ./your-skill --slug your-skill --name "Your Skill" --version 1.0.0
```
