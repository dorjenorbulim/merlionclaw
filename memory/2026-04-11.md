## 🏛️ Modern Socrates Framework — Created (April 11, 2026)

**Full framework document created for coaching practice.**

### Core Aspiration
> "To be like Socrates — without the hemlock."

### The Socrates Method (What to Emulate)

1. **Socratic Questioning** (The Elenchus)
   - Ask, don't tell
   - Expose contradictions gently
   - Guide to self-discovery
   - Sample: "What do you mean when you say...?" "How do you know this is true?"

2. **Intellectual Humility**
   - "I know one thing: that I know nothing"
   - Admit uncertainty openly
   - Model curiosity over certainty

3. **The Examined Life**
   - Daily journaling (memory files)
   - Weekly reviews
   - Meditation/mindfulness
   - Feedback loops

4. **Wisdom Over Wealth** (But Not Poverty)
   - Charge for wisdom (coaching fees)
   - Build abundance (multiple streams)
   - Wealth enables more teaching

5. **Teaching Through Dialogue**
   - 1:1 coaching, group programs
   - Written + video content
   - Digital legacy

### Protection Framework (Avoiding the Hemlock)

1. **Serve, Don't Threaten** — Help people grow, don't attack systems
2. **Build Allies, Not Enemies** — Community protects
3. **Wisdom + Skillful Means** — Truth with compassion (Right Speech)
4. **Multiple Platforms** — Diversification = security
5. **Legal + Ethical Operation** — Work within structures
6. **Written + Digital Legacy** — Words outlive you

### Daily Practice

**Morning (15 min):**
- Intention: "Today, I serve wisdom, not ego"
- Review principles
- Plan one Socratic conversation
- Check protection (serving vs threatening?)

**Evening (15 min):**
- Reflection: What questions did I ask?
- Memory capture to daily file

**Weekly (60 min):**
- Review week's memory files
- Create 1 LinkedIn post (Socratic theme)
- Develop 1 coaching tool/framework

### Coaching Session Template

**Opening:** "This is your time. I ask questions, not give answers."

**Exploration Flow:**
1. Clarify (What?)
2. Examine Assumptions (Why?)
3. Explore Consequences (Then what?)
4. Consider Alternatives (What else?)
5. Connect to Values (Who?)

**Closing:** "What insights are you leaving with? What will you do differently?"

### Metrics of Success

- Clients served: 100+ over 5 years
- Revenue from wisdom: Track annually
- Content created: 50 posts/year
- Students mentored: 20+ young folks
- Legacy assets: Books, courses, frameworks
- Family: Sons financially literate + examined life
- Personal practice: Daily reflection, weekly review
