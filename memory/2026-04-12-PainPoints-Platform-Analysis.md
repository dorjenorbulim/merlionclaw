### Core Features:

### Monetization Models:

#### 1. **Freemium (Recommended for Launch)**
- **Free:** Post pain points, browse, upvote, basic advice
- **Premium ($9.99/month):**
  - Priority responses (48-hour guarantee)
  - Verified expert access
  - Unlimited questions
  - Ad-free experience
  - Advanced search/filters

#### 2. **Bounty System**
- Users attach bounty ($10-500) to pain points
- Helpers compete to provide best solution
- Platform takes 20% commission
- Bounty released when solution is marked "Solved"

#### 3. **Expert Marketplace**
- Verified professionals (lawyers, doctors, financial advisors)
- Set hourly rates ($50-300/hour)
- Platform takes 15-25% commission
- Video/voice consultations booked through platform

#### 4. **B2B Insights (High Value)**
- Sell anonymized pain point data to companies
- "Top 100 Pain Points in Singapore — Q2 2026"
- Pricing: $5,000-50,000 per report
- Customers: Product teams, startups, government agencies

#### 5. **Sponsored Challenges**
- Companies sponsor specific pain point categories
- "DBS Pain Point Challenge: Personal Finance"
- Prize pool: $10,000-50,000
- Platform fee: 20% of prize pool

#### 6. **Affiliate/Referral Revenue**
- Recommend products/services that solve pain points
- Earn affiliate commission (5-20%)
- Example: "Best insurance for [pain point]" → affiliate link

## ⚠️ Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: **Low Initial Engagement**
- **Mitigation:** Seed with 100+ pain points before launch
- **Mitigation:** Pay 10-20 "founding members" to post and respond daily
- **Mitigation:** Partner with influencers/communities for launch

### Risk 2: **Low-Quality Advice**
- **Mitigation:** Verified expert badge (require credentials)
- **Mitigation:** Community rating system (upvote/downvote)
- **Mitigation:** Report inappropriate content (moderation team)

### Risk 3: **Legal Liability (Bad Advice)**
- **Mitigation:** Terms of service (advice is informational, not professional)
- **Mitigation:** Disclaimers on all posts
- **Mitigation:** Verified experts carry their own insurance
- **Mitigation:** Platform does not guarantee advice quality

### Risk 4: **Monetization Challenges**
- **Mitigation:** Start with freemium + bounties (lowest friction)
- **Mitigation:** B2B insights (high-value, low user friction)
- **Mitigation:** Iterate pricing based on user feedback

### Risk 5: **Competition from Reddit/Quora**
- **Mitigation:** Singapore-specific focus (local context)
- **Mitigation:** Verified local experts (Quora doesn't have this)
- **Mitigation:** Bounty system (Quora doesn't pay advisors)
- **Mitigation:** Integration with SG services (government, local businesses)
