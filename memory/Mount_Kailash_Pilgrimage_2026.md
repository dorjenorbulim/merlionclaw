## Altitude Preparation

### Acclimatization Strategy
**Before Tibet:**
- No specific altitude training (you're in Singapore, sea level)
- Focus on **aerobic fitness** (stronger base = better altitude tolerance)

**In Tibet (Critical):**
- **Arrive Lhasa (3,650m):** 2-3 days acclimatization
- **Drive to Darchen (4,600m):** 1-2 days rest
- **Start kora:** Only after feeling good at Darchen

**Acclimatization Signs:**
- ✅ Good: Normal energy, sleeping well, no headache
- ⚠️ Warning: Mild headache, slight nausea (rest, hydrate)
- ❌ Danger: Severe headache, vomiting, confusion (descend immediately)

**Prevention:**
- Ascend slowly (don't rush)
- Hydrate aggressively (4-5L water/day)
- Avoid alcohol at altitude
- Consider Diamox (consult doctor)
