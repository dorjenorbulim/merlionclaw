# Card Tribe Research Pack — ABORTED

**Date:** April 11, 2026

**Reason:** Competitive landscape — similar concept already launched

---

## Background

The "Card Tribe Research Pack" was a proposed trading card marketplace research/analysis product.

## Why Aborted

**Competitor Discovery:** www.thetcgmarketplace.com was established in 2026 with a similar concept.

**Key Learnings:**
- Market validation: TCG marketplace concepts have demand (someone else is doing it)
- Timing: They launched first this year
- Differentiation needed: Would need unique angle to compete
