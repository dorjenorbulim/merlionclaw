## ⚠️ Important Warnings

### What NOT to Do:
- **General Fashion Rental** — Style Theory closed (high ops costs)
- **Car Subscription (Solo)** — ZipZap already in SG, COE complexity
- **Health/Medical Devices** — HSA approval required (slow, expensive)
- **Copying Without Localizing** — Must adapt for SG culture, pricing

### What TO Do:
- **Niche Down** — Don't do "everything," pick one category
- **Start Small** — 20-50 units, prove model, then scale
- **Partner Early** — Suppliers, distributors, local businesses
- **Localize** — SG pricing, culture, preferences matter
- **Launch Fast** — 30 days, not 6 months
