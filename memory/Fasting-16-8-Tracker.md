## Subhuti's Fasting Notes

### What Breaks a Fast:
- ✅ Water, black coffee, plain tea (0 calories)
- ❌ Any food, even small amounts
- ❌ Sweetened beverages
- ❌ Milk/cream in coffee
- ❌ Bone broth (technically breaks fast)

### Breaking Fast Tips:
1. **Don't binge** — Your stomach has shrunk
2. **Start with protein** — Helps satiety
3. **Chew thoroughly** — Aids digestion
4. **Hydrate first** — 1-2 cups water before eating

### Common Challenges:
- **Hour 14-16:** Hunger peaks — drink water, stay busy
- **Hour 18-20:** Energy may dip — normal adjustment
- **Sleep:** May take 3-7 days to normalize

### When to Break Fast Early:
- ☐ Dizziness that doesn't pass
- ☐ Nausea
- ☐ Severe headache
- ☐ Feeling faint
- ☐ Any concerning symptoms

**Always prioritize health over the schedule.**
