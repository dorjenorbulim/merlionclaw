## Purpose

This tracker helps you:
- **Record** what you're reading (titles, authors, progress)
- **Deepen** appreciation through focused questions
- **Connect** reading to ICF coaching competencies
- **Connect** reading to Buddhist practice (dharma lens)
- **Remember** key insights long-term

---

## How to Use

**When you start a book:**
1. Share title + author with Subhuti
2. Subhuti creates book entry with focused questions
3. Read with those questions in mind
4. Share insights as you go (I'll log them)

**When you finish:**
1. Capture key takeaways
2. Note how it connects to ICF / Buddhism / life
3. Decide: Will you re-read? Apply something? Teach it?

## Reading Tips (Subhuti's Suggestions)

### Before Reading
- Set intention: Why this book?
- Skim first (table of contents, intro, conclusion)
- Have Subhuti prepare questions

### During Reading
- Read with questions in mind
- Note insights (I'll log them)
- Pause to reflect (don't rush)

### After Reading
- Summarize in your own words
- Identify 1-3 actionable takeaways
- Connect to what you already know
- Decide: Apply, re-read, or release?
