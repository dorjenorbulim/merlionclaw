## ⚡ QUICK SHOOT GUIDE

**What You Need:**
- Smartphone (4K if possible)
- Tripod (or stable surface)
- Good lighting (natural light or ring light)
- Quiet space (for voiceover)
- Editing app (CapCut, InShot, or Descript)

**Time to Produce:**
- Recording: 30 min
- Editing: 1-2 hours
- Total: ~2 hours

**Canva/Stock Footage Alternatives:**

- Canva (templates + effects)
- Envato Elements (paid, premium footage)

## 🚀 VIRAL OPTIMIZATION

**Hook (First 3 Seconds):**
- Must grab attention immediately
- Use pattern interrupt (unexpected visual or statement)
- "There's a frequency..." works because it's mysterious

**Retention (Middle):**
- Change visual every 3-5 seconds
- Use text overlays for key points
- Build tension (reveal gradually)

**Payoff (End):**
- Satisfying conclusion
- Clear CTA
- Emotional resonance (not just information)
