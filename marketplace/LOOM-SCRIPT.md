## 🎥 Recording Tips

### Before Recording

- [ ] Close all tabs except AgentHub
- [ ] Turn off notifications (Do Not Disturb)
- [ ] Test microphone (clear audio matters)
- [ ] Have script open (but don't read robotically)
- [ ] Smile. Be yourself. Authentic > perfect.

### During Recording

- **Pace:** Slow down (nervous = fast)
- **Energy:** Enthusiastic but not hype
- **Mistakes:** Pause, restart sentence (edit later)
- **Eye contact:** Look at camera (if face cam on)

### After Recording

- [ ] Trim start/end (remove dead air)
- [ ] Add title: "AgentHub — 2-Min Beta Demo"
- [ ] Add description: "Beta launches April 13. Apply now: [your URL]"
- [ ] Copy share link
- [ ] Embed in emails (or just link to Loom)
