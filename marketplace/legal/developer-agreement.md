# AgentHub Developer Agreement

**Effective Date:** April 8, 2026  
**Parties:** AgentHub Pte. Ltd. ("Platform") and Developer ("You")

---

## 1. Overview

This agreement governs your participation in the AgentHub marketplace as a developer of autonomous AI agents.

**Key Terms:**
- **You keep 70%** of all revenue from your agents
- **AgentHub takes 30%** for distribution, billing, and support infrastructure
- **You retain all IP** rights to your agents
- **Non-exclusive** — you can sell elsewhere simultaneously

---

## 2. Developer Obligations

### 2.1 Agent Quality

You agree to:
- Provide accurate descriptions of agent capabilities
- Maintain 99%+ uptime for hosted agents
- Respond to customer support tickets within 48 hours
- Fix critical bugs within 72 hours of report
- Update agents for compatibility with platform changes

### 2.2 Compliance

You agree to:
- Not create agents that violate laws (fraud, harassment, illegal activities)
- Not create agents that infringe on third-party IP
- Comply with data privacy regulations (GDPR, PDPA, etc.)
- Include appropriate disclaimers for agents providing advice (legal, medical, financial)

### 2.3 Transparency

You agree to:
- Disclose what data your agent collects
- Disclose if your agent uses third-party APIs
- Provide clear documentation for setup and usage
- Be transparent about limitations and edge cases

## 3. Revenue Share

### 3.1 Standard Split

| Revenue Type | Developer | AgentHub |
|--------------|-----------|----------|
| Subscription (monthly) | 70% | 30% |
| One-time purchase | 70% | 30% |
| Usage-based (per API call) | 70% | 30% |
| Enterprise contract | 70-80% | 20-30% |

### 3.2 Payment Terms

- **Payout Schedule:** Monthly (15th of each month for prior month)
- **Minimum Payout:** $100 (balances below $100 roll over)
- **Payment Method:** Bank transfer, PayNow, or Wise
- **Currency:** USD (converted to local currency at time of payout)

### 3.3 Refunds

- AgentHub offers 7-day money-back guarantee to customers
- Refunds deducted from your revenue share (pro-rated)
- Disputed refunds: AgentHub mediates, final decision within 7 days

## 4. Intellectual Property

### 4.1 Your IP

- **You retain all ownership** of your agents
- This agreement grants AgentHub a **non-exclusive license** to distribute
- You can sell your agents on other platforms simultaneously
- You can remove your agents from AgentHub with 30 days notice

### 4.2 AgentHub IP

- AgentHub retains all rights to the marketplace platform
- You receive a **non-exclusive license** to use AgentHub APIs and tools
- You cannot reverse-engineer or copy AgentHub's platform
- You cannot use AgentHub branding without written permission

### 4.3 Derivative Works

- If you create agents based on AgentHub-provided templates: **You own the agent**
- If AgentHub creates custom agents for you: **Negotiated separately**
- If we jointly develop an agent: **Written agreement required**

## 5. Support & Maintenance

### 5.1 Developer Responsibilities

- **Level 1 Support:** AgentHub handles (billing, access, basic troubleshooting)
- **Level 2 Support:** Developer handles (agent-specific bugs, feature questions)
- **Response Time:** 48 hours for non-critical, 24 hours for critical issues

### 5.2 AgentHub Responsibilities

- Provide developer dashboard (analytics, revenue, customer data)
- Handle billing, collections, chargebacks
- Provide API documentation and SDK
- Market the marketplace (drive traffic to your agents)
- Monitor for fraud and abuse

### 5.3 Updates

- **Minor Updates:** Developer pushes anytime (no approval needed)
- **Major Updates:** 7 days notice to existing customers
- **Breaking Changes:** 30 days notice, AgentHub approval required
- **Security Patches:** Immediate (no notice required)

## 6. Termination

### 6.1 By Developer

- You can terminate this agreement with **30 days written notice**
- Existing subscriptions continue until end of billing period
- You receive revenue share for all active subscriptions during notice period
- After 30 days: agents removed from marketplace, no further revenue

### 6.2 By AgentHub

- AgentHub can terminate for **material breach** with 14 days notice
- Immediate termination for: fraud, illegal activity, IP infringement
- Developer receives revenue share earned up to termination date
- No penalty for termination (except breach scenarios)

### 6.3 Effect of Termination

- Customers retain access until end of billing period
- Developer can offer to migrate customers to direct relationship
- AgentHub can offer alternative agents to affected customers
- No ongoing obligations after termination (except accrued payments)
