## 1. Beta Refund Terms

### 1.1 Free Trials (Beta)

During beta, all agents include:
- **14-day free trial** (no credit card required)
- **Full access** to all features
- **No obligation** to purchase after trial

### 1.2 Paid Subscriptions (Beta)

If you choose to purchase during beta:
- **7-day money-back guarantee** (full refund, no questions asked)
- **Pro-rated refunds** for monthly subscriptions (cancel anytime, refund for unused days)
- **No refunds** for usage-based billing after consumption (you pay for what you use)

## 2. How to Request a Refund

### 2.1 During Free Trial

**No action needed.** Simply stop using the agent. No charges will apply.

### 2.2 After Purchase (Within 7 Days)

**Email:** dorjenorbulim@gmail.com  
**Subject:** Refund Request — [Agent Name]  
**Include:**
- Your name and email
- Agent name
- Purchase date
- Reason for refund (optional, helps us improve)

**Response time:** Within 48 hours  
**Refund processed:** 5-10 business days

### 2.3 After 7 Days

**Contact:** dorjenorbulim@gmail.com  
**We'll review:** Case-by-case (discretionary)  
**Factors:** Technical issues, agent not as described, extenuating circumstances
