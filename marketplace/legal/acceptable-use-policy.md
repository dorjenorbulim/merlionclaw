## 1. Overview

This Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) describes prohibited activities on AgentHub.

**Violations may result in:**
- Warning (first offense, minor)
- Suspension (serious or repeat offense)
- Termination (severe or harmful offense)
- Refund forfeiture (if paid subscription)
- Legal action (if illegal activity)

---

## 2. Prohibited Activities

### 2.1 Illegal Activities

You may NOT use AgentHub to:

| Activity | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Fraud** | Scams, phishing, identity theft, financial fraud |
| **Money Laundering** | Structuring, layering, integration of illicit funds |
| **Terrorist Financing** | Funding designated organizations or individuals |
| **Sanctions Evasion** | Circumventing OFAC, UN, EU, MAS sanctions |
| **Cybercrime** | Hacking, malware, ransomware, DDoS attacks |
| **Drug Trafficking** | Selling controlled substances |
| **Human Trafficking** | Exploitation, forced labor, sex trafficking |
| **Weapons Trade** | Illegal arms, explosives, weapons of mass destruction |
| **Child Exploitation** | CSAM, grooming, child sexual abuse material |
| **Gambling** | Unlicensed gambling, betting, wagering (Singapore law) |

**If we suspect illegal activity:** We will report to authorities (MAS, SPF, etc.)

---

### 2.2 Harmful Content

You may NOT create, distribute, or promote agents that:

| Content Type | Examples |
|--------------|----------|
| **Hate Speech** | Racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, religious hatred |
| **Harassment** | Bullying, stalking, threats, doxxing |
| **Violence** | Glorification of violence, self-harm, suicide |
| **Misinformation** | Medical misinformation, election interference, conspiracy theories |
| **Adult Content** | Pornography, sexually explicit material (unless properly labeled) |
| **Spam** | Unsolicited bulk messages, deceptive practices |
| **Defamation** | False statements damaging to reputation |

**Gray areas:** We'll review case-by-case. When in doubt, email us.

---

You may NOT:

| Violation | Examples |

| **Trademark Infringement** | Using logos, names, brands without authorization |

### 2.4 Platform Abuse

You may NOT:

| Abuse Type | Examples |
|------------|----------|
| **Spamming** | Multiple accounts, fake reviews, manipulation |
| **Scraping** | Automated data extraction without permission |
| **Reverse Engineering** | Decompiling, disassembling, extracting agent code |
| **Security Bypass** | Circumventing authentication, access controls |
| **DoS Attacks** | Overloading servers, degrading performance |
| **Account Sharing** | Sharing login credentials (except team accounts) |
| **Fee Avoidance** | Trying to avoid AgentHub fees (off-platform transactions) |

**Technical violations:** We'll detect and ban automatically.

---

### 2.5 Professional Advice Without Disclaimers

Agents providing advice MUST include disclaimers:

| Advice Type | Required Disclaimer |
|-------------|---------------------|
| **Legal Advice** | "Not legal advice. Consult a licensed attorney." |
| **Medical Advice** | "Not medical advice. Consult a licensed healthcare provider." |
| **Financial Advice** | "Not financial advice. Consult a licensed financial advisor." |
| **Tax Advice** | "Not tax advice. Consult a licensed tax professional." |

**Missing disclaimer:** We'll request addition. Repeated violation = removal.

## 5. Enforcement Process

### 5.1 Detection

We detect violations via:
- User reports (email: dorjenorbulim@gmail.com)
- Automated monitoring (spam, malware, abuse patterns)
- Manual review (founder reviews flagged accounts)
- Developer/Customer complaints

### 5.2 Review

When we detect a potential violation:
1. **Investigation** (within 48 hours)
2. **Evidence collection** (logs, communications, agent code)
3. **Decision** (warning, suspension, termination)
4. **Notification** (email to affected user)

### 5.3 Appeal

If you disagree with our decision:
1. **Email appeal** (dorjenorbulim@gmail.com, subject: "AUP Appeal")
2. **Include:** Your account, violation, why you disagree, evidence
3. **Review:** Founder reviews within 7 days
4. **Decision:** Final (binding for beta period)
