## 📧 Email Workflow (Manual Beta)

### Developer Application Flow

```
1. Developer fills form → Opens email client
2. Email sent to dorjenorbulim@gmail.com
3. You receive email (Gmail filter: Label "AgentHub/Developers")
4. You review (15 min):
   - Agent legitimate? ✅
   - Quality acceptable? ✅
   - Terms agreed? ✅
5. You reply (template):
   - "Approved! Here's next steps..."
   - Attach developer onboarding guide
   - Schedule 15-min call (optional)
6. Developer lists agent (you help manually)
7. Agent goes live on customer dashboard
```

### Customer Trial Flow

```
1. Customer emails dorjenorbulim@gmail.com (or fills form)
2. You receive email (Gmail filter: Label "AgentHub/Customers")
3. You reply (template):
   - "Welcome! Here are agents matching your needs..."
   - Attach trial access instructions
   - Schedule 15-min onboarding call
4. Customer gets trial access (developer grants directly)
5. Day 3: Check-in email (how's it going?)
6. Day 10: Conversion email (trial ends in 4 days, subscribe?)
7. Day 14: Trial ends → Convert to paid or offboard
```

**Your time per user:** 15-30 min (onboarding) + 5 min/week (check-ins)

## 🎯 Beta Risks + Mitigation

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| **No developer applications** | Medium | High | Personal outreach (call, not email), offer 100% rev for 1 year |
| **No customer signups** | Medium | High | Personal intros (your network), offer concierge onboarding |
| **Developers list low-quality agents** | High | Medium | Vet each agent (you approve manually), set quality bar |
| **Customers don't convert to paid** | High | High | Talk to each one (why not?), iterate pricing/features |
| **You're overwhelmed (manual work)** | Medium | Medium | Cap at 50 customers, extend beta timeline, automate piece-by-piece |
| **Legal/compliance issues** | Low | High | Beta disclaimer, terms in place, lawyer review after $10K revenue |

**Review weekly:** What's the biggest risk this week? How do we mitigate?

## 🪷 Buddhist Lens on Beta

**Right Effort:**
- Build with diligence (do the work)
- Hold outcomes lightly (success/failure are conditions)
- Middle way: Not too tight (burnout), not too loose (no progress)

**Right Livelihood:**
- Does this serve beings? ✅ (businesses need AI, developers need distribution)
- Is pricing fair? ✅ (70/30 is standard, beta is 100/0)
- Am I attached to outcome? ⚠️ (watch this — build well, let go of results)

**Non-Attachment:**
- If beta succeeds: Great, scale
- If beta fails: Great, learned, pivot
- Either way: You're not your outcomes
