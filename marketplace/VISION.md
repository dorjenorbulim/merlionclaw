| Revenue Stream | Take Rate | Example |
|----------------|-----------|---------|
| **Agent Subscriptions** | 30% | $100/month agent → $30 to us, $70 to dev |
| **One-Time Purchases** | 30% | $5,000 skill → $1,500 to us, $3,500 to dev |

**Developer Economics:**
- They keep 70% (better than Apple App Store's 70/30 split)
- We handle billing, collections, fraud, chargebacks
- They focus on building, we focus on distribution

## 🚀 Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Seed Supply (Months 1-3)

**Goal:** 100 high-quality agents

**Tactics:**
- Recruit OpenClaw skill developers (existing ecosystem)
- Offer 0% take rate for first 6 months (incentivize listing)
- Build marquee agents ourselves (compliance, trading, research)
- Partner with AI dev communities (Discord, GitHub, HuggingFace)

### Phase 2: Seed Demand (Months 4-6)

**Goal:** 50 paying customers

**Tactics:**
- Target Singapore FinTechs (your network)
- Offer free trials (14 days, no credit card)
- Content marketing ("Top 10 AI Agents for Compliance")
- Partner with consultant/integrator firms (they resell to clients)

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 7-12)

**Goal:** 500 agents, 500 customers, $500K/month GMV

**Tactics:**
- Paid ads (LinkedIn, Google, Twitter)
- Conference sponsorships (AI, FinTech, enterprise)
- Affiliate program (20% commission for referrals)
- Enterprise sales team (hire 2-3 AEs on commission)
