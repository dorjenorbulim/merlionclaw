## Executive Summary

Subhuti AI Coach targets the gap between expensive human coaching platforms (BetterUp at $3K-5K/user/year) and basic wellness apps. We offer AI-powered mindfulness and burnout prevention coaching at 1/10th the price, designed specifically for Asia-Pacific SMEs in tech, professional services, and healthcare.

**Value Proposition:** Enterprise-grade coaching accessibility for companies that can't justify BetterUp budgets but need real burnout support.

1. **Always mention BetterUp first** in sales conversations: "Companies like yours typically pay $3K-5K per user per year for human coaching through BetterUp..."

3. **Frame as "AI-first" not "AI-only":** We're not replacing human coaches; we're making coaching accessible to everyone, not just executives.

**ROI Justification Framework:**

| Metric | Calculation | Example (300 employees) |
|--------|-------------|-------------------------|
| **Burnout Cost** | 15% of staff × $80K avg salary × 50% replacement cost | 45 employees × $80K × 50% = $1.8M/year |
| **Our Solution Cost** | $2,200/month × 12 | $26,400/year |

- Employee launch communication (template provided)

- Baseline survey: burnout index, engagement score

**Phase 2: Active Usage (Week 3-8)**
- Weekly usage reports to HR/L&D lead

**Phase 3: Evaluation (Week 9-12)**

- Executive debrief (60 min): metrics, testimonials, ROI

- ≥40% of employees activate account (log in + 1 session)

## 5. Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Beta Customers Don't Engage

**Probability:** Medium (30%)  
**Impact:** High (no case studies, no revenue)

**Prevention:**
- **Executive sponsorship:** Require CEO/HR Director to send launch email to employees
- **Manager involvement:** Train managers to model usage (they use it first, then encourage teams)
- **Launch campaign:** Provide email templates, Slack announcements, posters (digital)
- **Incentives:** Gamification (streaks, badges), team challenges, small prizes (gift cards)

**Intervention (if activation <25% after 2 weeks):**
- 1:1 calls with 5-10 employees (understand barriers)
- A/B test different launch messages
- Add manager dashboard (see team activation rates)
- Offer "office hours" — live Q&A sessions

**Exit Strategy:**
- If activation <15% after 4 weeks despite interventions → Offer to pause pilot, relaunch later
- Document learnings: Was it timing? Communication? Product fit?

### Risk 2: AI Responses Aren't Helpful

**Probability:** Medium (25%)  
**Impact:** High (churn, negative word-of-mouth)

**Prevention:**
- **Rigorous testing:** 100+ conversation scenarios before launch
- **Human review:** Sample 10% of conversations weekly for quality
- **Feedback loop:** "Was this helpful?" thumbs up/down after each session
- **Escalation path:** If AI can't help, offer to connect with human coach (partner network)

**Intervention (if helpfuleness score <70%):**
- Analyze low-rated conversations: common patterns?
- Retrain model on high-rated conversations
- Add conversation guardrails (avoid vague advice)
- Bring in domain experts (psychologists, coaches) to review responses

**Exit Strategy:**
- If helpfulness <60% after 3 iterations → Delay public launch, invest in model improvement
- Be transparent with customers: "We're improving the model, here's our timeline"

### Risk 3: Privacy Concerns Arise

**Probability:** Low-Medium (20%)  
**Impact:** High (trust erosion, legal risk)

**Prevention:**
- **Privacy by design:** No individual conversations visible to admins
- **Data residency:** Host data in Singapore (AWS/GCP SG region)
- **Compliance:** PDPA (Singapore), GDPR (if EU employees), HIPAA (if healthcare)
- **Transparency:** Clear privacy policy, data usage FAQ for employees

**Intervention (if concerns raised):**
- Offer security review call with CTO/technical lead
- Provide data flow diagram + encryption details
- Offer on-premise deployment option (Enterprise tier)
- Third-party security audit (SOC 2 Type I within 6 months)

**Exit Strategy:**
- If major customer demands data access → Politely decline (core to product design)
- If regulatory issue arises → Engage legal counsel immediately, pause affected feature

### Risk 4: Competitors Launch Similar Product

**Probability:** Medium-High (40%)  
**Impact:** Medium (pricing pressure, differentiation challenge)

**Competitive Landscape:**
- **BetterUp:** Launching AI features (but core is human coaching, expensive)
- **Risely:** AI coaching for managers (~$100-200/user/year)
- **Valence:** AI mental health coach (consumer-focused, not enterprise)
- **Coachello:** Singapore-based, leadership coaching (human + AI hybrid)

**Differentiation Strategy:**
1. **Asia-Pacific focus:** Culturally-aware coaching (mindfulness, Buddhist-inspired, local examples)
2. **SME pricing:** 50% cheaper than Risely, 95% cheaper than BetterUp
3. **Burnout specialization:** Not generic coaching — specifically designed for burnout prevention
4. **Speed to market:** First-mover advantage in Singapore SME segment

**Intervention (if competitor launches):**
- Accelerate public launch (don't wait for perfect product)
- Lock in beta customers with 12-month contracts (20% discount)
- Double down on customer success (high NPS = defensibility)
- Build network effects: Employee referrals, manager advocacy

**Exit Strategy:**
- If well-funded competitor enters with 10x lower pricing → Pivot to niche (healthcare, legal, high-stress industries)
- If competitor acquires key talent → Focus on customer relationships (harder to replicate)
