## ⚠️ Red Flags — Stop & Rest If:

- Sharp pain in lower back (not muscle soreness)
- Pain radiates down leg (sciatica)
- Numbness or tingling
- Pain persists >48 hours after exercise
- Sleep disrupted by pain

**Rule:** "Good pain" = muscle soreness (24-48 hours). "Bad pain" = sharp, shooting, persistent.
